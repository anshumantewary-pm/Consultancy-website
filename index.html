About the Practice

Strategy without pretence. Results without compromise.


For over a decade, I have worked with organisations ranging from early-stage ventures to global enterprises — helping leadership teams cut through noise, align on what truly matters, and execute with conviction.

My approach is rooted in first-principles thinking, radical honesty, and a deep respect for the complexity of real organisations. I don't deliver slide decks. I deliver change.

My Approach

How we work together.

01

Deep Discovery

Before any recommendation, I invest time genuinely understanding your organisation — its history, culture, unspoken tensions, and unrealised potential.

02

Strategic Clarity

I help you identify the few decisions and actions that will create the most meaningful change — and give you the courage to say no to everything else.

03

Sustained Execution

Strategy without implementation is fiction. I stay alongside you through the hard work of change — coaching, adjusting, and holding the vision.
